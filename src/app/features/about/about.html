About Me

Building scalable, user-centered web applications with Angular

Hello! I'm Luka Chemia — Front-End Angular Developer

I’m a Front-End Developer with 2+ years of experience building data-driven and scalable Angular applications. I’ve worked on over 10 admin-side and 3 client-side projects for Aldagi and its subsidiaries, improving performance and user experience across platforms.

My technical expertise includes Angular, TypeScript, RxJS, NgRx, and modern styling frameworks like SCSS, Tailwind, and Angular Material. I also specialize in integrating REST APIs, secure authentication with Keycloak, and designing UX/UI systems in Figma.

I’m passionate about clean, maintainable code, team collaboration, and continuous learning. I enjoy turning complex data into intuitive, visually appealing user interfaces that make a real impact.
